MOTIVATION¶

Although there are many websites provides, such as typing exercises or typing speed measurement, But none of this allows the article to be chosen by yourself. I think typing can strengthen our memory, especially for languages, which you are not good to read. This game not only helps you increase your typing speed but also help you review your articles repeatedly. That is my motivation for developing this game.
